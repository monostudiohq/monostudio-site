Privacy Policy

HushPad · Mono Studio · Effective: March 20, 2026

Overview

HushPad is an ambient sound generator designed for sleep and focus. We believe in simplicity and transparency. This policy explains what data we collect — which is none.

Data Collection

HushPad does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account or login required
  • No personal information collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data
  • No cookies or device identifiers

Audio Processing

All audio is generated and played locally on your device. HushPad does not require an internet connection to function. No audio data is recorded or transmitted.

Advertising

The current version of HushPad does not contain any advertising. If we introduce advertising in a future update (e.g., Google AdMob), we will update this privacy policy to disclose any advertising identifiers or data collection associated with the ad service.

Children's Privacy

HushPad does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@monostudio.app.

概述

HushPad 是一款環境音產生器，專為睡眠與專注設計。我們重視簡潔與透明，以下說明我們收集的資料 — 答案是零。

資料收集

HushPad 不收集、儲存或傳輸任何個人資料：

所有音訊都在您的裝置本地產生與播放。HushPad 不需要網路連線即可運作，不會錄製或傳輸任何音訊資料。

政策變更
